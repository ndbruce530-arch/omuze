Omuze — Herero Family Lineage App

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Omuze account directly from within the app. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the Omuze app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (tap the gear icon).
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Read the confirmation message and tap Delete Permanently.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials (email and password).
  • Your profile information (display name and profile photo).
  • Your authentication session and all login tokens.
Please note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, you will need to create a new account if you wish to use Omuze again.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance deleting your account, please contact us at omuze.app@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and we will process your request within 7 business days.
